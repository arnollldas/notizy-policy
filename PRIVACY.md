## What data is stored?

Notizy stores your notes (title, content, date, pinned status)
exclusively on your local device via the browser's storage feature
(`chrome.storage` / local browser storage).

**There is no server and no cloud.** Your notes never leave your
device and are never transmitted to the developer, third parties,
or any external service.

The only exception is the optional feedback feature (see below).

## What is the data used for?

Solely to display and edit your notes within the extension. There
is no analytics, no tracking, and no data sharing of any kind.

## How long is the data stored?

Until you delete a note yourself or uninstall the extension.
Uninstalling the extension automatically and completely removes all
locally stored notes from your device.

## Feedback feature

If you submit a message through Notizy's feedback form, only the
text you entered is sent by email to the developer (via the EmailJS
service). No note contents, usage data, or other personal data are
sent automatically.

## Your rights

Since no data is stored outside your device, you can control your
data fully and at any time — by deleting individual notes or by
uninstalling the extension.
